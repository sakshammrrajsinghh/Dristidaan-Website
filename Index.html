Meet Dr. S. K. Mitra

M.B.B.S, M.S (Ophthalmology)

After completion of his medical degrees from Assam Medical College, Dr. Mitra served at M. N. Chatterjee Memorial Eye Hospital in Kolkata. Moving to Jamshedpur in 1994, he took charge of the Eye Department of Tata-Tinplate Hospital, establishing it as a premier Phaco Centre in Jharkhand.

In 2012, he established his own advanced setup, Drishtidan Phaco Centre at Golmuri, which has since earned a reputation as the Advanced Phaco Surgical centre of the region.

At Drishtidan Phaco Centre, Dr. S.K. Mitra has introduced "No Injection Cataract Surgery." Most cases are performed under Topical Anesthesia—meaning only anesthetic eye drops are used. Patients remain completely at ease with no fear of injections!

Dr. Mitra is a life member of AIOS, OSA, JHOS, EIZOC, and IMA. With over 50 publications and presentations in national and international conferences, he is a dedicated educator and pioneer in spreading awareness about advanced cataract surgery.

Our Advanced Facilities & Procedures

Advanced PhacoSurgery

Utilizing the world-acclaimed Sovereign machine with WhiteStar ICE and Fx. technology. Nearly 100% of our cataract cases are handled with this state-of-the-art procedure.

Multifocal & Trifocal IOLs

Guided by modern innovation, we implant Multifocal, Presbyopic, and Trifocal Intra Ocular Lenses to provide clear Far, Intermediate & Near vision post-surgery.

High-Tech Diagnostics

Our centre is fully equipped with an Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) machine, Yag Laser, Fundus Photography, and Automated Perimetry (IVS-201B) machines.
